Game Introduction

Advance Wars is a turn-based tactical strategy game developed by Intelligent Systems and published by Nintendo for the Game Boy Advance in 2001. The game puts players in command of an army with the goal of defeating enemy forces using strategic positioning and tactical decision-making. Players control various military units including infantry, tanks, artillery, and air and naval units, each with their own strengths and weaknesses. The game features multiple commanding officers (COs) with unique abilities that can turn the tide of battle when used effectively. With its accessible gameplay but deep strategic elements, Advance Wars became a beloved classic in the strategy genre.

Controls & Tips

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Move cursor
  • Z Key: Select/Confirm
  • X Key: Cancel/Back
  • A Key: Menu
  • S Key: Information
  • Enter: Start/Pause

Game Tips

  • Capture cities to increase your funds each turn
  • Use terrain to your advantage - forests provide defense bonuses
  • Infantry units are essential for capturing properties
  • Build a balanced army with different unit types
  • Save your CO Power for critical moments in battle
  • Pay attention to unit strengths and weaknesses
  • Repair damaged units at cities or bases
