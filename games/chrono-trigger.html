Game Introduction

Chrono Trigger is a classic role-playing game released by Square in 1995, acclaimed as one of the greatest RPGs of all time. The game tells the story of protagonist Crono and his companions as they travel through different time periods to prevent the world's destruction. The game is known for its innovative battle system, multiple endings, and deeply moving storyline, representing a pinnacle achievement in JRPGs.

Controls & Tips

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Move character
  • Z Key: Confirm/Talk/Attack
  • X Key: Cancel/Menu
  • A Key: Special skills
  • S Key: Switch character

Game Tips

  • Make good use of Dual and Triple Techs for more efficient battles
  • The same location in different time periods may have different treasure chests
  • The game has multiple endings depending on your choices
  • In New Game+ mode, you can challenge the final boss earlier
  • Look out for hidden characters and weapons
