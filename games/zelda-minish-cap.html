Game Introduction

The Legend of Zelda: The Minish Cap is an action-adventure game released by Nintendo for the Game Boy Advance in 2004. The game tells the story of Link and his companion, a magical talking cap named Ezlo, as they embark on a quest to save the petrified Princess Zelda. The game's most distinctive feature is Link's ability to shrink to a tiny size, allowing him to explore areas inaccessible at normal size and communicate with the Minish people. The game incorporates classic Zelda elements while introducing new gameplay mechanics and items.

Controls & Tips

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Move Link
  • Z Key: Use sword/Confirm
  • X Key: Use shield/Cancel
  • A Key: Item 1
  • S Key: Item 2
  • Q Key: Open map
  • W Key: Open inventory

Game Tips

  • Find shrinking portals to become miniature size
  • Collect Kinstones to fuse with other characters
  • Explore dungeons to obtain important items and Heart Containers
  • Complete side quests for additional rewards
  • Use your shrinking ability to discover hidden secrets
  • Learn various sword techniques for more effective combat
